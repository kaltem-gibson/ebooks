The Three Fundamentals of Telekinesis

Success in telekinesis is not achieved through physical strength, but through the mastery of three mental frequencies as I have outlined:

Focus (Fundamental I): I define this strictly as "no thought loudness". Unlike traditional concentration, true focus occurs when you silence internal thought echoes at the brow chakra.

Energy (Fundamental II): I define this as "matter in its pure form". This fundamental is associated with the crown chakra and represents your internal sensation and production of PSI energy.

Force (Fundamental III): The final execution stage, which I locate at the crown chakra. I guide the application of Force through three secondary components: Sight, Emotion, and Tension.

Energetic Safety and Aura Quality

I emphasize that telekinesis cannot be safely or effectively practiced while in a "bad aura" state. In my findings, I provide specific markers for determining your energetic suitability:

Determining a Good Aura: I encourage practice only if you possess a calm mind, peaceful environment, and lack of physical tension or reliance on mind-altering substances.

The Warning Signs: I have identified indicators of a negative state which include "thought loudness," recurring nightmares, ringing in the left ear, and unexplained physical discomforts like stabbing pains or migraines.

The Environmental Limitation: A critical discovery I present in this volume is the "Wind Theory," which I demonstrate through the sealed jar experiment. I prove that telekinesis cannot influence matter in an environment completely devoid of internal energy in motion, reinforcing my principle that mind and environment must act as a single, harmonious system.
